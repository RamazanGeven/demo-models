Many textures in this directory come from
public domain textures from unfinished game "Golgotha":
http://www.wolfiesden.com/golgotha/golgotha.asp
(previously on http://www.jonathanclark.com/, but they don't seem
available there anymore).

016marbre, 17_gris_vert, (probably) bois5 from
LeMog - 3d Textures:
http://www.lemog.fr/lemog_textures/index.php

tree_sprite rendered from a model
"Tree leave LowPoly", by Moser Juan Jose, CC-BY 3.0, from
http://opengameart.org/content/tree-leave-lowpoly

test_texture.png based on this photo
http://gimp-savvy.com/cgi-bin/img.cgi?ufwsMV1VJR1LXOE702
from Copyright-Free Photo Archive on
http://gimp-savvy.com/PHOTO-ARCHIVE/
(public domain).

bricks2.jpg from
http://opengameart.org/content/brick-texture
GPL >= 2.0, CC-BY-SA >= 3.0.

leaf* textures based on
"Maple leaf on concrete" photo by Martin LaBar,
http://flickr.com/photos/martinlabar/2072745893/

lion bump mapping textures from
Philippe David steep parallax mapping code,
http://www-static.cc.gatech.edu/grads/d/davidp/parallax_mapping/ ,
based on paper about steep mapping:
http://graphics.cs.brown.edu/games/SteepParallax/index.html

blood_in_the_eyes texture based on a fragment of
Poppy background by Pieter Krul (GNU LGPL)
http://ftp.gnome.org/pub/GNOME/teams/art.gnome.org/backgrounds/NATURE-Poppy_1024x768.jpg
from art.gnome.org nature backgrounds:
http://art.gnome.org/backgrounds/nature
